About us

Our story

In 2017 we were fortunate enough to acquire a small vineyard with a beautiful house and garden near Naumburg in the Saale-Unstrut region. Since then we have taken care of the 35 vines, which we can increase to up to 100, as we are registered in the European vineyard register. Taking care of vines and cultivating them has become an integral part of our lives.

Chasselas / Winzervereinigung

The white grape variety Chasselas is 5,000 years old and is therefore one of the oldest of all grape varieties. The grape variety was cultivated in Germany at the start of the 17th century, and it is now grown outside of the Saale-Unstrut region in Markgräflerland. Wines of Chasselas are mild and go perfectly with light dishes. We deliver our grapes to the Winzervereinigung Freyburg-Unstrut, where they are processed into wine.

Wethau / Saale-Unstrut

The Saale and Unstrut area is a place where an ancient European cultural landscape is joined by the age-old craft of winemaking. Passing through mediaeval castles and fortresses, the two rivers, which give their names and character to a landscape with gentle hills and slopes, flow past until they naturally merge together.
